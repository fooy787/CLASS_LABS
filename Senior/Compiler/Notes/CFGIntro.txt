Brakus Nauger Form (BNF form).

if-> IF LP ID EQ NUM RP LB stmts RB e
e-> else LB stmts RB | lambda

ID ::= <\w+>
NUM ::= <\d+>
IF ::= \bif\b
ELSE ::= \belse\b


D -> \d+
A -> [-+*/]
s-> D| S A D | lambda | M
M -> DDDDD

OUTPUT
S 4
M 1
5 S -> D D D D D
