                     <h2>
                        
                           Yost Labs Among 2017 Best University Startups
                        
                     </h2>
                  </div>
               </div>
            </div>
         </div>
         <div class="post">
            <div class="container">
               <div class="row">
                  <div class="section-content span9 pull-right">
                     <div class="callout">
                        <h4>Date</h4>
                        <p>
                           
                              
                              			June 7, 2017
                              		
                           
                        </p>
                        <h4>Information</h4>
                        <p>
                           
                              
                              			Yost Labs was among 40 University Startup companies selected to present in Washington,
                              DC.
                              		
                           
                        </p>
                     </div>
                     
                        
                        <p>Yost Labs was among 40 University Startup companies selected to present to Fortune
                           1000 corporations, policy leaders, federal government agencies, angel investors, and
                           entrepreneurs in Washington, DC as part of <a href="https://ncet2.org" target="_blank">University Startups Conference &amp; Demo Day</a>.
                        </p>
                        <p><img src="/media/startups.jpg" alt="2017 Best University Startups" width="1200" height="800"></p>
                        <p>The event featured universities and partners with innovative applied research as well
                           as internship opportunities. “As a university partner, it was an honor to be invited
                           to talk about our relationship with Yost Labs, describe our game development programs,
                           and introduce Greg Merril, CEO of Yost Labs, who shared highlights of products they
                           are developing with the help of faculty, students, and resources here at SSU,” SSU
                           President Rick Kurtz said.
                        </p>
                        <p>Shawnee State supports innovation and student and faculty collaborations.</p>
